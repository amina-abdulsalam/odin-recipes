Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice, a seasoned tomatoey rice dish, is eaten at every party, Sunday lunch, and everything in between.

Ingredients

  • Plum tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Red onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Long grain parboiled rice
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Blend the vegetables and transfer to a sauce pan
  2. Fry onion and spices with the oil
  3. Add the blended peppers to the onions
  4. Add rice and cook until done
