Oatmeal Recipe

Learn how to make oatmeal like a pro! It’s truly one of the easiest (and fastest) healthy meals you can make.

Enjoy a basic bowl of oatmeal or get creative with endless sweet and savory flavor options. I’ll give you a few ideas and tips!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Any desired toppings!

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, stir in oats, water and milk
  2. Let it cook until soft
  3. Add any desired toppings
