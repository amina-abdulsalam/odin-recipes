Pancakes Recipe

These pancakes are fluffy and super soft with the added bonus of no oil or butter!

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Milk
  • White flour

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl with a whisk
  2. Heat up a nonstick pan over medium heat
  3. Griddle a quarter cup of batter over the pan and fry
  4. Repeat until all batter has been used
